GY
George Young
Cybersecurity Expert & Adjunct Professor, NLU

The Role of AI in Cybersecurity

Transforming Defense and Redefining Threats

How AI is powering both cyber defense and cyber attacks in today's digital world.

George Young is a cybersecurity expert and educator with over 20 years of experience in enterprise security, networking, and technology strategy. He has served in leadership roles, including Chief Technologist and CISO, specializing in cybersecurity architecture, risk management, and secure infrastructure design.

He holds a Master of Science in Communication Systems from Northwestern University and has a strong foundation in both engineering and applied cybersecurity practices. George is also an Adjunct Professor at National Louis University, where he teaches cybersecurity and AI, helping students develop real-world skills for today's evolving threat landscape.

LinkedIn Rooms 4016 - 4016B
AV
Andrea Veiga Rincon
Senior Manager, Loyalty · Grubhub

How to Be the Essential Bridge Between Technical Work and Business Impact

Discover how to translate technical insights into business outcomes and become a trusted bridge between teams.

Andrea is currently a Senior Manager at Grubhub, focusing on Loyalty program strategy and growth. Her journey at Grubhub began in 2023 as a Senior Marketing Analyst for CRM strategy, where she focused on optimizing engagement for push and email communications.

After that, Andrea spent a year working in Market Strategy Growth at Grubhub, which led to her current role in Loyalty. Before joining Grubhub, Andrea honed her analytical skills at Bank of America, where she served as a Quantitative Analyst in Consumer Credit Risk and later transitioned into Credit Card Marketing after completing her 2-year rotational program.

She earned her degree in Economics and Statistics with a minor in Data Analytics from NC State University in 2020.

AI & Game Development

James "Quake" Cottrell III is a well-traveled technology instructor in Chicago who currently serves as an adjunct instructor at several for-profit schools, including City Colleges of Chicago, and has taught for over 15 years. He developed Project Fly approximately six years ago with an initial team of 20 developers, several of whom were his former students from his tenure at Sanford-Brown College.

Today, Cottrell is the founder of two companies, Quake Technology Inc. and Quake Studios LLC, both of which create opportunities for youth and adults in urban communities. Quake Technology Inc. is a mobile application development, digital content marketing, and augmented reality firm. Quake Studios LLC produces in-house mobile video games, feature films, and 3D animation.
